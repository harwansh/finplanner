# SmartFinly Privacy Notice

Last updated: 2026-04-25

## 1. Purpose

SmartFinly processes the information users enter to generate an educational AI financial planning output.

## 2. Data users should not enter

Users must not enter sensitive identifiers such as PAN, Aadhaar, bank account numbers, IFSC, UPI IDs, OTPs, passwords, access tokens, or documents containing such data.

## 3. Data processed

The planner may process:
- age and family profile,
- income and salary components,
- expenses and liabilities,
- insurance cover amounts,
- investment and SIP information,
- goals and target years,
- tax deduction inputs.

## 4. Use of data

The data is used to:
- calculate financial estimates,
- generate AI planning output,
- show tax, insurance, goal and investment summaries,
- apply safety and compliance guardrails.

## 5. Storage

Unless a separate persistence layer is added, the frontend planner does not intentionally store user profiles in an account. Backend logs should not record full financial profiles or sensitive identifiers.

## 6. AI processing

Inputs may be processed by the configured AI model/provider to generate educational outputs. Users should avoid entering personal identifiers.

## 7. User rights and requests

If a persistence layer, login, analytics, or document upload is added later, SmartFinly should add a formal request process for access, correction, deletion, grievance contact, and consent withdrawal.

## 8. Children's data

Do not enter personally identifying details of children. Use generic names like "Child 1" if possible.

## 9. Contact

Add business contact / grievance contact before public launch.
