# Legal and Regulatory Compliance Checklist

Last updated: 2026-04-25

## Current product positioning

SmartFinly should be positioned as:

> Educational AI financial planning sample for Indian households.

It should not be positioned as:
- SEBI-registered investment adviser,
- SEBI-registered research analyst,
- RBI-approved lending product,
- insurance broker,
- tax filing service,
- portfolio management service,
- stock/mutual fund recommendation engine,
- guaranteed-return product.

## SEBI risk checklist

SEBI materials describe investment advisers as SEBI-registered professionals who provide personalized financial guidance. SEBI's Investment Adviser regulations also say that no person should act as or hold itself out as an investment adviser without registration unless exempt.

Avoid:
- "we advise you to buy/sell",
- named stock/fund/security recommendations,
- target returns as guarantees,
- model portfolios sold as personalized advice,
- paid personalized securities advice unless registration/compliance is completed.

## Research analyst risk

Avoid publishing research-style buy/sell/hold calls, price targets, or security-specific recommendations unless Research Analyst compliance is reviewed.

## RBI risk checklist

The current planner should not include lending, loan distribution, credit scoring for lenders, loan approval, repayment collection, or loan marketplace flows without reviewing RBI digital lending and outsourcing requirements.

Avoid:
- "RBI approved",
- "instant loan approval",
- collection of bank credentials,
- loan execution or loan servicing flows.

## Data protection / privacy checklist

India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 framework focuses on lawful purpose, notice, consent / legitimate use, and data principal rights. Before public launch or persistence:
- publish privacy notice,
- avoid unnecessary personal data,
- collect only what is needed,
- add consent notice,
- add deletion/access request process,
- avoid sensitive identifiers,
- avoid logging full profiles,
- review AI provider data flow.

## Insurance risk checklist

Do not sell, solicit, compare or rank insurance products unless distribution/broking compliance is reviewed.

Allowed educational scope:
- estimate life cover need,
- estimate health cover gap,
- explain assumptions.

Avoid:
- "buy this policy",
- insurer ranking,
- premium collection,
- claim guarantee.

## Tax risk checklist

Tax output should be called an estimate. Users should verify with a tax professional before filing or declaring payroll investments.

## Required website copy

Every output should include:
- educational only,
- not SEBI/RBI registered advice,
- no guaranteed returns,
- no product execution,
- verify with qualified professionals,
- do not enter PAN/Aadhaar/bank/OTP/password.

## Pre-launch legal actions

1. Add business entity name.
2. Add contact/grievance email.
3. Publish Terms and Privacy pages.
4. Confirm whether any SEBI/RBI/IRDAI registration is required for planned monetization.
5. Review AI provider terms and data retention.
6. Review hosting/logging retention.
7. Review ad/marketing copy for prohibited claims.
