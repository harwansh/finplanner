# SmartFinly Terms of Use

Last updated: 2026-04-25

## 1. Educational use only

SmartFinly is an educational and sampling tool. It uses AI and calculation guardrails to help users understand personal finance concepts, cash-flow, tax estimates, insurance gaps, goal planning, and investment affordability.

SmartFinly is **not** a SEBI-registered Investment Adviser, SEBI-registered Research Analyst, RBI-regulated lending platform, insurance broker, tax filing service, portfolio manager, mutual fund distributor, stock broker, or legal advisor.

## 2. No regulated advice

The output is not investment advice, securities advice, research advice, tax advice, legal advice, insurance advice, lending advice, or a recommendation to buy/sell/hold any security, mutual fund, insurance product, loan product, or financial instrument.

Users must consult qualified and appropriately registered professionals before taking financial action.

## 3. No product execution

SmartFinly does not execute transactions, collect investment money, place orders, sell insurance, sell loans, manage portfolios, or guarantee outcomes.

## 4. No guarantees

All projections are estimates. Returns, tax outcomes, inflation, income growth, market conditions, insurance underwriting, and user circumstances can change. SmartFinly does not guarantee any return, tax saving, goal achievement, or financial outcome.

## 5. User responsibility

Users are responsible for:
- verifying all input data,
- reviewing assumptions,
- validating tax calculations with a tax professional,
- validating investment and insurance decisions with qualified professionals,
- not entering sensitive identifiers.

## 6. Prohibited data

Do not enter:
- PAN,
- Aadhaar,
- bank account number,
- IFSC,
- UPI ID,
- OTP,
- password,
- access token,
- documents containing sensitive identifiers.

## 7. AI limitations

AI output can be incomplete, inaccurate, outdated, or unsuitable for a specific situation. SmartFinly uses guardrails, but users must independently verify all outputs.

## 8. Limitation of liability

To the fullest extent permitted by law, SmartFinly and its operators are not liable for losses arising from reliance on educational outputs, assumptions, projections, AI responses, or user-entered data.

These terms may be updated as the product evolves.
